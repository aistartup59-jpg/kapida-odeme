# ADR-003: QR Architecture

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-09

## Context

QR-based payment is a core product feature. The meaning of "QR" must be defined precisely to avoid incorrect implementations that use payment links disguised as QR codes.

## Decision

QR always means a real bank payment QR (TR Karekod / EMV QR).

QR must never be generated from a Payment Link.

Flow:

1. Employee generates a bank QR.
2. Customer opens their banking application.
3. Customer scans the QR.
4. Payment succeeds via the banking network.
5. The PaymentRequest is updated via webhook.

## Consequences

- QR implementation requires a bank or payment provider that supports TR Karekod / EMV QR generation.
- QR and Payment Link remain independent features with separate flows.
- Provider adapters must implement `generateBankQR` as a distinct method.

## Alternatives Considered

- Generating QR from a payment link: rejected because it does not produce a real bank QR and misleads the customer experience.
