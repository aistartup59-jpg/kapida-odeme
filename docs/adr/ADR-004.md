# ADR-004: Payment Method vs Delivery Channel

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-09

## Context

SMS and WhatsApp are sometimes conflated with payment methods. The domain model must distinguish how a payment is made from how a payment link is delivered to the customer.

## Decision

PaymentMethod and DeliveryChannel are separate concepts.

PaymentMethod values:

- QR
- PAYMENT_LINK
- NFC
- CASH

DeliveryChannel values:

- NONE
- SMS
- WHATSAPP
- COPY_LINK

SMS and WhatsApp are delivery channels only. They are not payment methods.

## Consequences

- A Payment Link can be delivered via SMS, WhatsApp, or copied — these are orthogonal choices.
- The API accepts `paymentMethod` and `deliveryChannel` as independent fields.
- Reporting and filtering can independently query by method or channel.

## Alternatives Considered

- Treating SMS and WhatsApp as payment methods: rejected because they are delivery mechanisms, not payment settlement methods.
